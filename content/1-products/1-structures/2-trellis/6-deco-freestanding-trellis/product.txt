Overview: 

This 70 inch trellis, with its signature art deco styling, gives character and definition to your entryway, porch or patio areas. The trellis unfolds to a 90-degree angle and provides ample support for vines or other climbing plants. For superior strength, this trellis is constructed using premium-grade, thick cedar lath and heavy-duty, pre-installed hinges. The sturdy pot base provides sufficient elevation to allow for water drainage and helps minimize patio staining. Trellis requires no staking when used with potted plant.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 24.5" x 15.5" x 70"
