Overview: 

The Glendale is a modestly priced version of the popular arched-style arbor. This arbor features airy lattice side panels that provide open support for vines or roses. For lasting strength the arches are constructed with finger-jointed clear cedar with no metal fasteners to stain the wood. The pre-assembled side panels feature furniture-style, mortise and tenon joinery of rail to posts. Lattice panels are double fastened at every joint and set into channels in the surrounding frame with bleed-free hardware. Packaged in a single carton and complete with all hardware and easy-to-use instructions. Includes 4 steel anchors for securing arbor with cement.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 41.25" x 27" x 86"
Inside Opening: 36.5"
