Overview: 

The Astoria is a handsome pergola-style arbor, with its bow-shaped header and well-scaled lattice side panels. This arbor also converts easily into a charming garden seat with the addition of our optional Arbor Seat with Back (sold separately). For lasting strength, the pre-assembled side panels feature furniture-style, mortise and tenon joinery of rail to posts. Lattice panels are double fastened at every joint and set into channels in the surrounding frame with bleed-free hardware. Packaged in a single carton and complete with all hardware and easy-to-use instructions. Includes 4 steel anchors for securing arbor with cement.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 64" x 29" x 86.25"
Inside Opening: 42.5"
