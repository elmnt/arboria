Overview: 

A truly elegant arbor combining a soft curve of the header with the traditional angularity of the gable. The Craftsman Style features add a custom touch and suggest use as an entry or gateway. The Nantucket offers authentic architecture and quality construction at a modest price. Large Gable header supports heavy vines and foliage. The 47-inch clear opening accommodates wide paths. For lasting strength, the pre-assembled side panels feature furniture-style, mortise and tenon joinery of rail to posts. Lattice panels are double fastened at every joint and set into channels in the surrounding frame with bleed-free hardware. Packaged in a single carton and complete with all hardware and easy-to-use instructions. Includes 4 steel anchors for securing arbor with cement.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 72.75" x 29" x 97"
Inside Opening: 47.5"
