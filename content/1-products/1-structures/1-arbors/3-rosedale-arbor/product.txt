Overview: 

To accommodate varying path widths, the Rosedale has an adjustable opening width with pre-drilled settings of 36, 42 and 48 inches. For lasting strength, the pre-assembled side panels feature furniture-style, mortise and tenon joinery of rail to posts. Lattice panels are double fastened at every joint and set into channels in the surrounding frame with bleed-free hardware. Packaged in a single carton and complete with all hardware and easy-to-use instructions. Includes 4 steel anchors for securing arbor with cement.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 64" x 29" x 84"
