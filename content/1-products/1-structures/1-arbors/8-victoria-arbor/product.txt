Overview: 

The Victoria provides a luxurious, slender entrance to a special place. Designed with smaller spaces in mind, the 36.5 inch clear opening is framed with handsome, finely milled side-panels. For improved stability, the Victoria features smoothly jointed arches mounted directly to edge-molded ledger boards. Airy lattice side panels act as the perfect support for your choice of climbing plants. For lasting strength the arches are constructed with finger-jointed clear cedar with no metal fasteners to stain the wood. The pre-assembled side panels feature furniture-style, mortise and tenon joinery of rail to posts. Lattice panels are double fastened at every joint and set into channels in the surrounding frame with bleed-free hardware. Packaged in a single carton and complete with all hardware and easy-to-use instructions. Includes 4 steel anchors for securing arbor with cement.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 41.25" x 27" x 87"
Inside Opening: 36.5"
