Overview: 

Add a touch of elegance to your garden or landscape. The Andover Arch features a finely milled arched cedar frame with finger jointed, furniture style construction. A closely spaced lattice pattern is used to create the body panel--offering privacy without fencing. Includes two 3-ft. metal stakes for easy installation.
