Overview: 

The Andover Wing wins praise for its performance as a supporting role to the Andover Arch (sold separately). Featuring a closely spaced lattice pattern which offers privacy without fencing. Andover Wings are sold in pairs. Includes two metal stakes for easy installation. May be used as a separate garden accent, or use the included hinges to attach to the sides of the Andover Arch (sold separately).

Assembled dimensions (w x d x h): 22.5" x 1.375" x 47.5"
