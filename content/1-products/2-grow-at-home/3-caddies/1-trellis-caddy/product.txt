Overview: 

Useful for indoor and outdoor locations, the Trellis Caddy has limitless functions. The tray is designed to accommodate three 5” pots, while the trellis back is both decorative and functional - pin important notes or photos on the trellis. Three utility hooks (included) attach to hold keys, small garden tools, or even a dog leash.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 19” x 6” x 20”
