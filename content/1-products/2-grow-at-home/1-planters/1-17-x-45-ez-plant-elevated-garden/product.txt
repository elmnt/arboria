Overview: 

This elevated planter box is designed to allow gardeners to tend to their plants while standing instead of kneeling on the ground. The box is crafted with tongue and groove bed walls set into mortised corner posts for added strength. Tapered legs and beveled post tops make it a decorative, yet practical, addition for patios and decks. A storage shelf at the base proves useful for storing small tools and gardening materials. This model is wider than our other EZ Plant Elevated Garden, yet more compact to fit shallow balconies and along railings. Both EZ Plant Elevated Gardens hold a similar amount of soil. 2 cubic foot capacity/15 gallons.

Assembled dimensions (width x depth x height): 45" x 17" x 32"
